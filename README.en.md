# Cybersecurity Labs

Repository of **hands-on cybersecurity laboratories**, focused on **experimentation, guided learning, and technical training**, with a strong emphasis on **education, ethics, and responsibility**.

This project is mainly designed for **middle and high school students**, teachers, and anyone interested in entering the world of **information security**, combining theory, practice, and analysis.

---

## Objectives

- Understand the **fundamental principles of cybersecurity**
- Explore **real vulnerabilities in controlled environments**
- Analyze **attacks and defenses from a technical perspective**
- Develop **security awareness and critical thinking**
- Encourage **secure programming best practices**
- Introduce concepts of **digital forensics, malware analysis, and ethical hacking**

## Project Philosophy

This repository **does not promote illegal hacking**.

All content is developed under the principles of:

- **Education**
- **Research**
- **Responsibility**
- **Professional ethics**

The goal is to **understand how attacks work in order to better prevent them**.

> *"The best way to defend a system is to understand how it can be attacked."*
